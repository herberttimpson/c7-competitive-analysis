Project Overview

Circle 7 Beef is going direct-to-consumer. The storefront lives at circle7meat.com — a custom-built HTML site on Cloudflare Pages. Commerce (checkout, payments, order management) runs on the From The Farm (FTF) platform at shop.circle7meat.com.

The business is owned by Justin (Circle 7 Livestock, LLC). Herbert and Joseph are not owners — they build and run the storefront, product catalog, and marketing backend.

The Pitch
Steakhouse quality. Ranch price.

"The quality your favorite steakhouse charges $90 for — raised on our ranch in Central Utah, shipped direct to your door."
